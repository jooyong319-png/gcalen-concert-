Trivium have revealed a stacked fall 2026 headline tour, teasing new music and fresh material live. The run opens November 5 in St. Petersburg, Florida and closes December 19 in Charlotte, North Carolina.

Support comes from In Flames, Orbit Culture, Fit For An Autopsy and Frozen Soul across all 31 dates. A Blabbermouth presale (code "BLABBER") runs July 22–23, with general on-sale Friday, July 24 at 10 a.m. local time.
