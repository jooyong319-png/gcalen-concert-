ZZ Top have rescheduled two dates on their ongoing The Big One! Tour so Billy Gibbons can appear at Eric Clapton's Crossroads Guitar Festival in Austin on September 26–27.

The Nashville show originally set for September 26 now moves to October 8, while the September 27 date in Port Wentworth, Georgia shifts to October 9. Existing tickets remain valid for the new dates.
