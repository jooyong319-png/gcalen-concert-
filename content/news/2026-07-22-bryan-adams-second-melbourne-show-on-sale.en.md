After his March 5 Rod Laver Arena show sold out, Bryan Adams has added a second Melbourne date for March 7, 2027, on his Roll With The Punches tour of Australia and New Zealand. Natalie Imbruglia joins as special guest.

Presales for the new show begin Friday, July 24, ahead of the general on-sale Monday, July 27 at noon local time. The Frontier Touring run opens February 23 in Perth.
