Multi-platinum singer-songwriter Gayle has confirmed her debut full-length album, Observing Chaos, will arrive September 18 via Atlantic Records. The reveal came alongside a new single, "Loving You Is," a stripped-back, piano-led ballad about self-worth.

Following last month's "Junebug!," the record digs into love, heartbreak and growing up between ages 17 and 21. Gayle also plans a fan-focused road trip of album listening sessions ahead of release day.
