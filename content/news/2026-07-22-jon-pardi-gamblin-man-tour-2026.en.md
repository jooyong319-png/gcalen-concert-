Jon Pardi has unveiled the Gamblin' Man Tour 2026, a 19-date US arena and amphitheater run named after a cut from his fifth album, "Honkytonk Hollywood." It opens September 17 in Richmond, Virginia and wraps November 14 in Independence, Missouri.

Alexandra Kay and Sadie Bass provide support. Tickets go on sale Friday, July 24 at 10 a.m. local time, following an artist presale that began Tuesday, July 21.
