Mike D 5D have expanded their 2026 schedule with 20 fresh dates across the US, Canada, the UK and Europe. The added leg begins September 20 in Santa Ana, California and runs through November 5 in Brussels, hitting Los Angeles, Seattle, Toronto, London and Amsterdam.

Public tickets go on sale Friday, July 24 at 10 a.m. local time, with an early mailing-list sale first. The tour lands alongside Mike D's debut solo LP, "Thank You," due August 28.
