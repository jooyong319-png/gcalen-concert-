Latin Grammy winner Silvana Estrada has mapped a North American run behind her acclaimed album Vendrán Suaves Lluvias. It opens Aug. 8 at San Francisco's Outside Lands and includes headline dates in Portland, Vancouver, Miami Beach and Nashville, plus support slots on Mon Laferte's tour.

Select-market presales began July 22 at 10 a.m. local time, with general on-sale following July 24. Estrada also joins Rubén Blades at the Hollywood Bowl on Sept. 30.
