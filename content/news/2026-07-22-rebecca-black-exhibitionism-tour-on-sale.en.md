Rebecca Black's 27-date "Exhibitionism on Tour – Rebecca Black as: Miss Delicious" opens Sept. 23 at Boston's Royale and wraps Nov. 5 at the Fonda Theatre in Los Angeles, with stops in New York, Atlanta, Chicago and more.

Artist and local presales ran July 22–23, closing at 11:59 p.m. on the 23rd. General on-sale begins July 24 at 10 a.m. local time. The run backs her second album, Age of the Exhibitionist, due Sept. 4.
