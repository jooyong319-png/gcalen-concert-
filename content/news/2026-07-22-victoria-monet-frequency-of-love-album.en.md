Victoria Monét revealed her sophomore album, Frequency of Love, will land October 2 via Lovett Music/RCA. To mark the announcement, she released lead single "Reach Out," produced by KAYTRANADA and D'Mile, alongside a Dave Meyers–directed video.

The 22-track record follows her Grammy-winning breakthrough Jaguar II. Fans can catch her this summer supporting Bruno Mars at London's Wembley Stadium before the album arrives in the fall.
