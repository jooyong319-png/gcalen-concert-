Doja Cat has confirmed Latto as special guest for the North American leg of her Tour Ma Vie World Tour. The Live Nation-backed run spans 31 arena dates, opening October 1 at Detroit's Little Caesars Arena and closing December 1 at Madison Square Garden in New York.

The North American leg follows a run through Asia, Latin America, Europe, the UK, Australia and New Zealand that produced multiple sold-out stops. Latto returns to the road for the first time since 2024, following her collaboration with Doja Cat on "Okayyy." Tickets are available now at dojacat.com.
